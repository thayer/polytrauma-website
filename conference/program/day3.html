Detailed Program

Tuesday, December 5, 2006

Day 1 > Day 2 > Day 3

On the morning of Day Three, at the conclusion of the conference, participants will report on the prior evenings work and outline the next steps to be taken in the development of the Polytrauma program. This discussion will include not only further specification of the program itself, but also the planning of a
proposal for the acquisition of funds to support the program. The conference will terminate at noon. All presentations will be held in Cummings 200 in the Thayer School of Engineering.
